About Us: Our Mission

Welcome to The Healing Farm! A service animal resource and community - partnering with Americans with Disabilities, American Kennel Club and many others to provide certified therapy animals and emotional support animals to the public. We also provide merchandise in our online Market to equip you and your gentle friends with the supplies to help you both succeed. Our mission is connecting therapy pets to the humans that need them most.

Located in Cincinnati, Ohio - we are an environmentally sound space where we train, adopt and rehabilitate therapy animals to earn industry certifications. Our dogs earn the coveted AKC (American Kennel Club) titles, through participation in the STAR Puppy and Canine Good Citizen programs. Our animals enhance quality of life and independence.Our mission is to connecting therapy pets to the people that need them most.
