About Us: Our Mission

Welcome to The Healing Farm! A service animal resource and community - partnering with Paws for a Cause, Americans with disabilities, American Kennel Club, Guide Dog Federation, Assistance Guides International and Love on a Leash to provide certified therapy animals and emotional support animals to the public. We also provide merchandise to equip you and your gentle friends with the supplies to help you both succeed.Our mission is connecting therapy pets to the humans that need them most.
